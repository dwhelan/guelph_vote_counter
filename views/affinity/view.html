Council Affinity

This visualization is based on how frequently councillors have voted alike. This is based on motions in 2014 with unanimous motions excluded.

For a mayoral race respective, what is clear is that Farbridge is the most influential leader here as she has the most number of 'connections'. It is interesting that the Guthrie has much less alignment with other councillors. The implication being that a Guthrie lead council would be more divisive.
